# EVID 620 — Conclusive Presumptions Defined

## Text
The presumptions established by this article, and all other presumptions declared by law to be conclusive, are conclusive presumptions.

## Why it Matters
This section defines the class of presumptions that are irrebuttable as a matter of law.

## War Room Use
- Identifies issues that cannot be contested through rebuttal evidence
- Prevents wasted strategy on facts the law treats as conclusively established
- Helps distinguish conclusive presumptions from rebuttable presumptions

## Related CACI
- Depends on the substantive claim and whether a conclusive presumption applies to a required issue

## Notes
Conclusive presumptions are legal closures, not mere evidentiary advantages.
