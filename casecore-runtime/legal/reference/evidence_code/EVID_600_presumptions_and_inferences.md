# EVID 600 — Presumptions and Inferences

## Text
(a) A presumption is an assumption of fact that the law requires to be made... A presumption is not evidence.  
(b) An inference is a deduction of fact that may logically be drawn...

## Why it Matters
Separates legal presumptions from actual evidence.

## War Room Use
- Prevents treating assumptions as proof
- Enables strategic use of presumptions

## Notes
Presumptions ≠ evidence. This is foundational.
