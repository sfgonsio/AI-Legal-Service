# EVID 355 — Limited Admissibility of Evidence

## Text
When evidence is admissible as to one party or for one purpose and is inadmissible as to another party or for another purpose, the court upon request shall restrict the evidence to its proper scope and instruct the jury accordingly.

## Notes
- Governs evidence admitted for a limited purpose or against a limited party.
- Requires restriction to proper scope and corresponding jury instruction upon request.

## Platform Relevance
- Important for intake classification where an artifact may be usable for one issue but not another.
- Supports downstream admissibility and instruction logic.
