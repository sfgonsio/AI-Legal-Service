# EVID 663 — Marriage Validity Presumption

## Text
A ceremonial marriage is presumed to be valid.

## Why it Matters
Establishes a proof-shifting presumption in favor of the validity of ceremonial marriage.

## War Room Use
- Relevant in family, succession, standing, and relationship-status disputes
- Narrows the burden on the party relying on ceremonial marriage validity

## Related CACI
- Case-specific depending on the role of marital status in the dispute

## Notes
Short section, but legally powerful when marriage validity matters.
