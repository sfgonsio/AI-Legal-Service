# EVID 522 — Burden of Proof for Insanity

## Text
The party claiming that any person, including himself, is or was insane has the burden of proof on that issue.

## Why it Matters
This section allocates the burden of proof to the party asserting insanity.

## War Room Use
- Primarily relevant when insanity or mental-state defenses/issues arise
- Supports issue-level burden allocation

## Related CACI
- Depends on the mental-state instruction at issue

## Notes
Likely low relevance in many civil commercial matters, but canonical where implicated.
