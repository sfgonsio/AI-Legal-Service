# EVID 660 — Presumptions Affecting Burden of Proof Defined

## Text
The presumptions established by this article, and all other rebuttable presumptions established by law that fall within the criteria of Section 605, are presumptions affecting the burden of proof.

## Why it Matters
Defines the category of rebuttable presumptions that shift the burden of proof.

## War Room Use
- Identifies presumptions that force the opposing party to disprove the presumed fact
- Distinguishes proof-shifting presumptions from production-only presumptions

## Related CACI
