# EVID 662 — Legal Title Presumption

## Text
The owner of the legal title to property is presumed to be the owner of the full beneficial title. This presumption may be rebutted only by clear and convincing proof.

## Why it Matters
Creates a strong ownership presumption and imposes a heightened rebuttal standard.

## War Room Use
- High-value in ownership and title disputes
- Forces a strong rebuttal showing against record title
- Important for burden and remedy analysis where property interests matter

## Related CACI
- Potentially relevant to ownership, title, and property-related instructions

## Notes
This is one of the most strategically powerful burden-of-proof presumptions in property disputes.
