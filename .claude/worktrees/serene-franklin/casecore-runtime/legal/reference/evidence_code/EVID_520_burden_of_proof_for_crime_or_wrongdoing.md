# EVID 520 — Burden of Proof for Crime or Wrongdoing

## Text
The party claiming that a person is guilty of crime or wrongdoing has the burden of proof on that issue.

## Why it Matters
This section allocates the burden of proof to the party asserting wrongdoing.

## War Room Use
- Important when fraud, wrongdoing, misconduct, or improper conduct is alleged
- Helps allocate proof responsibility on wrongdoing-related issues
- Supports issue-specific burden mapping

## Related CACI
- Potentially relevant to fraud and other wrongdoing-based instructions depending on case posture

## Notes
This should be applied at the issue level, not as a broad abstract rule.
