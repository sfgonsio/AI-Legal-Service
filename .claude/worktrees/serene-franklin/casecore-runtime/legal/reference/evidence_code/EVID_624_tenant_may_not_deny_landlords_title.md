# EVID 624 — Tenant May Not Deny Landlord's Title at Commencement of Relation

## Text
A tenant is not permitted to deny the title of his landlord at the time of the commencement of the relation.

## Why it Matters
This section establishes a conclusive rule in the landlord-tenant context regarding the landlord's title at the beginning of the relationship.

## War Room Use
- Relevant in landlord-tenant and title disputes
- Helps identify facts the tenant may be barred from contesting

## Related CACI
- Case-specific and typically limited to landlord-tenant/property issues

## Notes
Narrow but controlling where the landlord-tenant relationship is part of the dispute.
