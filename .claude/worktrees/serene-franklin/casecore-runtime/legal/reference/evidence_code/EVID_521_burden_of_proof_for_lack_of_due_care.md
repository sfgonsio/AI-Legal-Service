# EVID 521 — Burden of Proof for Lack of Due Care

## Text
The party claiming that a person did not exercise a requisite degree of care has the burden of proof on that issue.

## Why it Matters
This section allocates the burden of proof to the party asserting a failure to exercise due care.

## War Room Use
- Relevant in negligence and standard-of-care disputes
- Helps determine who must prove lack of due care
- Supports burden allocation for care-based issues
