# OUT OF SCOPE

## Purpose
Define what CASECORE must not do in MVP.

## Out of Scope
- autonomous legal advice
- autonomous legal conclusions
- unreviewed filing-ready outputs
- auto-promotion of AI-generated artifacts to canonical state
- unsupported case families beyond approved MVP scope
- direct AI writes to canonical database tables
- hidden confidence scoring presented as legal certainty
- marketing-first frontend behavior that obscures artifact state
- silent admin edits to canonical matter content
- unlogged override behavior

## Rule
Anything not explicitly approved in scope must be treated as out of scope until added through governed change control.
