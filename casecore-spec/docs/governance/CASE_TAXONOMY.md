# CASE TAXONOMY

## Purpose
Define the supported case taxonomy for CASECORE.

## MVP Scope
### Case Family
- Civil

### Perspective
- Plaintiff-first

### Initial Civil Subtypes
- breach_of_contract
- business_dispute
- partnership_dispute
- trademark_or_brand_dispute
- payment_or_invoice_dispute

## Future Reserved Expansion
- defendant perspective
- tort categories
- employment
- probate
- family
- criminal
- bankruptcy

## Rule
No unsupported subtype may be treated as first-class MVP behavior until explicitly added through governed change control.
