# LOCKED DECISIONS

## Purpose
Record the decisions that are closed and may not be casually reopened.

## Locked Decisions
1. Internal technical system name is `casecore`.
2. `/casecore-spec` is the authoritative source-of-truth directory.
3. Builder-facing execution materials must be derived from `/casecore-spec`.
4. Antigravity owns platform architecture and engineering execution.
5. Claude is a bounded proposal/intelligence subsystem, not the system architect.
6. Canonical artifacts may not be directly mutated by AI services.
7. Proposal artifacts must be clearly distinct from canonical artifacts.
8. Approval/promotion is a governed platform action.
9. Audit logging is mandatory for all material transitions.
10. Civil plaintiff-oriented litigation workflow is the MVP starting point.
11. Modular monolith is the acceptable MVP architecture unless superseded by ADR.
12. Internal technical naming may not include external branding.

## Change Rule
Any attempt to change a locked decision requires:
- a written ADR
- impact analysis
- update of affected contracts
- update of affected traceability records
- approval by product owner
