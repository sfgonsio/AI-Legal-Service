## 📊 NEW SLIDE ADDED

### SL-18: TrialForge Litigation Workflow Map (`/workflow-map`)

**Purpose:** Visual journey map showing where TrialForge operates across the complete litigation lifecycle

**Layout:** Horizontal timeline with 9 litigation stages from client intake through post-trial appeals

#### The Nine Litigation Stages:

1. **Client Intake**
   - Capture case narrative and initial evidence
   - Artifacts: Interview transcript, Matter record, Initial facts, Evidence uploads
   - Description: Documents and client information flow into TrialForge

2. **Early Case Assessment**
   - Strategic analysis and case theory development
   - Artifacts: Fact records, Event timeline, Case theory, Damages estimate
   - Description: Timeline graphics appear from structured facts

3. **Pleadings**
   - Initial court filings and case framing
   - Artifacts: Complaint, Answer, Counterclaims, Amended pleadings
   - Description: Legal documents assemble from arguments

4. **Discovery**
   - Evidence exchange and information gathering
   - Artifacts: Interrogatories, Requests for Production, Requests for Admission, Subpoenas
   - Description: Evidence network graphic emerges

5. **Depositions**
   - Witness testimony and evidence building
   - Artifacts: Deposition outlines, Exhibits, Deposition transcript, Deposition summary
   - Description: Transcripts transform into structured evidence nodes

6. **Motion Practice**
   - Pre-trial legal arguments and rulings
   - Artifacts: Motion to dismiss, Motion to compel, Summary judgment, Opposition briefs
   - Description: Arguments form legal reasoning structures

7. **Trial Preparation**
   - Evidence organization and presentation planning
   - Artifacts: Witness lists, Exhibit lists, Trial briefs, Jury instructions
   - Description: Evidence maps to witnesses and exhibits

8. **Trial**
   - Courtroom presentation and advocacy
   - Artifacts: Admitted exhibits, Trial transcript, Court rulings, Verdict
   - Description: Evidence flows into courtroom proceedings

9. **Post-Trial / Appeal**
   - Judgment and appellate proceedings
   - Artifacts: Judgment, Notice of appeal, Appeal brief, Appellate decision
   - Description: Record supports appellate arguments

#### Interactive Features:

- **Responsive Timeline:** Horizontal flow on wide screens, 3-column grid on tablets, vertical list on mobile
- **Hover Details:** Stage cards expand to show full descriptions and all artifacts
- **Color Coding:** Blue theme with navy accents throughout
- **Number Badges:** Each stage has prominent numbered badge
- **Power Grid:** TrialForge Intelligence Engine displayed below timeline showing 5-layer architecture support

#### "WOW Enhancement":

**TrialForge Power Grid** positioned beneath the 9-stage timeline with the message:

> "TrialForge Litigation Intelligence Engine: Transforming Evidence into Legal Strategy Across the Entire Case Lifecycle"

Shows the 5 layers (Evidence, Artifact, Program, Agent, Human) as supporting infrastructure beneath all workflow stages.
