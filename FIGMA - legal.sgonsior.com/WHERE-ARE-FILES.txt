═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                      ⚠️  ABOUT WIX HOSTING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Regular Wix DOES NOT support custom React apps like this.

❌ Can't upload "dist" folder to Wix
❌ No FTP access on Wix
❌ Wix builder doesn't work with React

✅ Solution: Use Netlify (free) or a subdomain
   - Keep main site on Wix
   - Portal on Netlify at: portal.yourcompany.com

📖 Read: WIX-HOSTING-HELP.md for full explanation
