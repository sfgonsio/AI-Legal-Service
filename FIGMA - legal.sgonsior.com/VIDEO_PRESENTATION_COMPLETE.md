11. **Deposition Preparation** (9s) - Tree diagram with question sequences
12. **Deposition Execution** (9s) - Processing pipeline animation

### **ACT 5: Trial & Knowledge**
13. **Trial Readiness** (8s) - Stacked exhibits with authentication checks
14. **Knowledge Evolution** (10s) - DIKW transformation with expanding rings
15. **Finale** (8s) - Floating particles with closing message
