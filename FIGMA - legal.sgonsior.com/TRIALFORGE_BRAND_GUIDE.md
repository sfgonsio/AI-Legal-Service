## 💡 BRAND MEANING

### The "Forge" Metaphor:

**TrialForge** represents an intelligent manufacturing process:

1. **Raw Materials (Evidence)** → Enter the forge
2. **Heat & Pressure (Processing Layers)** → Transform the materials
3. **Craftsmanship (AI + Human)** → Shape the final product
4. **Finished Artifacts (Legal Documents)** → Court-ready output

### Why "TrialForge" Works:

✅ **Memorable:** Single-word brand name  
✅ **Evocative:** Suggests transformation and precision  
✅ **Legal:** "Trial" indicates courtroom focus  
✅ **Process:** "Forge" implies systematic manufacturing  
✅ **Quality:** Forging suggests strength and durability  
✅ **Differentiated:** Not "AI Legal Assistant" or generic SaaS name

## 📊 CORE ARCHITECTURE

### The TrialForge Intelligence Engine (5 Layers):

**Layer 5 — Human Decision Layer**
- Review, Strategy, Approval
- Attorneys maintain control

**Layer 4 — AI Agent Layer**
- Legal Reasoning & Drafting
- Bounded AI assistance

**Layer 3 — Deterministic Processing Layer**
- Structured Legal Analysis
- Repeatable programs

**Layer 2 — Litigation Artifact Layer**
- Structured Case Intelligence
- Organized repository

**Layer 1 — Evidence Layer**
- Source of Truth
- Cryptographic verification

### The TrialForge Intelligence Flow (7 Stages):

1. Evidence → Raw information
2. Fact Extraction → Structured facts
3. Event Construction → Timeline
4. Legal Element Mapping → COA analysis
5. Legal Reasoning → Arguments
6. Litigation Artifacts → Documents
7. Court Process → Filing

### The TrialForge Lifecycle (9 Phases):

1. Client Intake
2. Early Case Assessment
3. Pleadings
4. Discovery
5. Depositions
6. Motion Practice
7. Trial Preparation
8. Trial
9. Post-Trial / Appeal

## 🔑 KEY DIFFERENTIATORS

### 1. Five-Layer Governance

**Not:** Black-box AI  
**Instead:** Transparent layer separation with attorney control at top

### 2. Evidence-Centered Architecture

**Not:** Document-first or AI-first  
**Instead:** Evidence → Facts → Events → Elements → Arguments

### 3. Litigation-Grade Traceability

**Not:** "Trust the AI output"  
**Instead:** Every artifact traces to source evidence with cryptographic hashing

### 4. Full Lifecycle Coverage

**Not:** Point solution for one workflow stage  
**Instead:** Client intake through post-trial appeals

### 5. Bounded AI with Deterministic Programs

**Not:** Pure AI everywhere  
**Instead:** Deterministic programs (Layer 3) + Bounded AI (Layer 4) + Human control (Layer 5)

## 📝 WRITING STYLE GUIDE

### Voice & Tone:

**Professional, Not Academic:**
- Use: "TrialForge transforms evidence"
- Not: "The system facilitates evidentiary processing"

**Confident, Not Arrogant:**
- Use: "Attorneys remain in control"
- Not: "Obviously, lawyers are necessary"

**Precise, Not Vague:**
- Use: "Five-layer architecture"
- Not: "Multi-tiered approach"

**Active, Not Passive:**
- Use: "The platform structures evidence"
- Not: "Evidence is structured by the platform"

### Terminology:

**Preferred Terms:**
- TrialForge (always one word, title case)
- Litigation intelligence infrastructure
- Court-ready artifacts
- Evidence transformation
- Attorney-controlled
- Traceable and reproducible
- Five-layer architecture
- Bounded AI agents
- Deterministic programs

**Avoid:**
- AI lawyer
- Automated attorney
- Legal robot
- Magic AI
- Black box
- Fully automated
- Replaces lawyers

### Capitalization:

- **TrialForge** — Always capitalize, one word
- **Intelligence Engine** — Capitalize when part of "TrialForge Intelligence Engine"
- **Layer names** — Capitalize: "Evidence Layer", "Program Layer"
- **Slide IDs** — Uppercase: "SL-16", not "sl-16"
