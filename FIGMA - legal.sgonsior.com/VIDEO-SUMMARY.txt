📹 ADDING YOUR REAL VIDEO
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

When you have your video file:

OPTION 1: Local Video File
1. Put video in: public/videos/your-video.mp4
2. Update videoUrl in VideoPresentation.tsx
3. Set useVideoPlayer to true

OPTION 2: YouTube
1. Upload to YouTube
2. Get embed code
3. Replace video element with iframe

OPTION 3: Vimeo/Other
1. Upload to service
2. Update videoUrl with hosted URL
3. Done!
