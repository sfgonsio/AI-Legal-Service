# Interview Methodology — Contract v1 (Two-Pass, Burden-Driven)

**Status:** SSOT (authoritative)  
**Purpose:** Define how INTERVIEW_AGENT conducts intake in a legally-grounded, client-trustworthy manner.

---

## 1. Goals

1. Collect facts in plain language.
2. Normalize facts into signals with citations.
3. Propose archetype hypotheses transparently.
4. Drive questioning based on missing signals (not guesswork).

---

## 2. Two-Pass Intake

### Pass 1: Neutral Fact Capture (Always)
Collect:
- who is involved (parties + relationships)
- what happened (timeline of events)
- where/when (time/place)
- what harm occurred (damages)
- what documents exist (contracts, emails, texts, filings)
- what the client wants (goals, outcomes)

**Rule:** no claim labels; no legal advice.

### Pass 2: Archetype Triage + Disambiguation
Using only Pass 1 signals:
- propose top 3 archetype hypotheses
- ask targeted disambiguation questions to confirm/refute

**Rule:** “working hypothesis” language + visible confidence.

---

## 3. Gap-Driven Questioning (Burden-Informed)

Once an archetype is selected (or highest-confidence hypothesis):
- derive burden map elements (from authority)
- map elements → required signals (pattern modules)
- ask questions that fill missing signals

This avoids bias from “similar cases” and prevents narrative hallucination.

---

## 4. Client Play-Back (Trust Layer)

Every major stage outputs two summaries:

### A) Client Summary (Plain English)
- what the system believes happened
- what the client is seeking
- uncertainties / missing info stated plainly

### B) Legal Working Map (Controlled Language)
- “This may involve X because we see signals A/B/C.”
- “To confirm, we still need D/E/F.”

**Rule:** the system invites correction; it does not argue.

---

## 5. Do-Not-Do Rules

INTERVIEW_AGENT must not:
- assert definitive claims or outcomes
- draft legal documents or arguments
- use prior cases to bias intake questioning
- “fill in” missing facts

---

## 6. Acceptance Criteria

Interview is correct when:
- it produces sufficient signals to drive archetype triage
- it captures missingness explicitly
- client play-back reduces disagreement by being transparent
- it can handle no-document intakes as the dominant path
